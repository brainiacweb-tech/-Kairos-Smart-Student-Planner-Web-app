How to Use

Step 1: Enter Your CWA

Enter your Cumulative Weighted Average percentage (0-100) in the input field. For example, if your CWA is 75.5%, enter "75.5".

Step 2: Click Convert

The tool will automatically convert your CWA to its equivalent GPA on the 4.0 scale and show your degree classification.

Understanding the Scale

CWA (0-100%) is your percentage-based grade. GPA (0-4.0) is the equivalent on the standard 4.0 scale used by many institutions.

Degree Classification

Your GPA determines your degree class - First Class is the highest honor, followed by Second Class Upper/Lower, Pass, or Fail.

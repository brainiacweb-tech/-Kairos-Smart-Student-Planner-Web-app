Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Kairos Smart Student Planner ("Kairos", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our web application.

Information We Collect

Section 1

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and institution when you register.
  • Academic data: Assignments, schedules, grades, and notes you enter into the app.
  • Profile photo: If you upload a profile picture, it is stored locally in your browser.
  • Google account information: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. We do not receive your Google password.
  • Usage data: Basic information about how you interact with the app (page visits, feature usage).

How We Use Your Information

Section 2
  • To provide and operate the Kairos app and its features.
  • To personalise your experience (e.g., display your name and profile photo).
  • To send deadline reminders via browser notifications (only if you enable this).
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns.
  • To respond to support requests.

Data Storage

Section 3

Most of your data (assignments, schedules, settings) is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and does not leave your device unless you use backend features such as file import/export. File processing is performed server-side on our Render-hosted backend and files are not retained after processing is complete.

Google OAuth

Section 4

When you choose to sign in with Google, we use Google OAuth 2.0. We only request access to your basic profile information (name, email, profile picture). We do not access your Google Drive, Gmail, or any other Google services. Your Google credentials are never stored by Kairos — authentication is handled entirely by Google.

Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Cookies

Section 5

Kairos uses localStorage and sessionStorage (not cookies) to store your session and preferences locally in your browser. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

Data Sharing

Section 6

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Service providers: Render (hosting) processes requests on our behalf under their own privacy policy.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Data Retention

Section 7

Your locally stored data persists until you clear your browser data or use the "Clear All Data" option in Settings. Server-side file processing data is discarded immediately after the operation completes.

Children's Privacy

Section 8

Kairos is intended for students aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Rights

Section 9

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for browser notifications at any time via Settings.
  • Clear all app data via Settings → Clear All Data.

Changes to This Policy

Section 10

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

Section 11

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Kairos app or by visiting our homepage.

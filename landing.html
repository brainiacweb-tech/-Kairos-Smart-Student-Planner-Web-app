Features

Everything a student needs

From assignment tracking to GPA calculation - Kairos has all the tools built in, for free.

Assignment Tracker

Add, prioritise, and track assignments with due dates, progress bars, priority badges, and file attachments.

Weekly Planner

Drag-and-drop timetable view showing your classes, study sessions, and personal events side by side.

Timetable Upload

Upload your school timetable as an image or CSV and Kairos generates a personalised study plan for you.

Smart Reminders

Browser notifications at 48h, 24h, 12h, 3h, and 1h before every deadline so nothing slips through.

GPA & CWA Calculator

Track your grades per course, calculate your GPA, convert CWA scores, and visualise performance trends.

Built-in Office Suite

Word, Excel, PowerPoint editors plus full PDF tools - merge, split, compress, and convert without leaving the app.

How it works

Up and running in 2 minutes

No complicated setup. Just sign up, add your courses, and start planning.

1

Create your account

Sign up with your email or continue as a guest. No credit card, no setup fees.

2

Upload your timetable

Upload your school timetable as an image or CSV - Kairos reads your schedule and suggests study blocks automatically.

3

Add your assignments

Enter deadlines, estimated hours, and priority levels. Kairos tracks everything and notifies you before due dates.

4

Install it on your phone

Add Kairos to your home screen on Android, iOS, or desktop. Works offline - no app store download required.

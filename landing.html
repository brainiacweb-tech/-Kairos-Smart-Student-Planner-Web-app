Features

Everything a student needs

From assignment tracking to GPA calculation — Kairos has all the tools built in.

Assignment Tracker

Add, prioritise, and track all your assignments with due dates, progress bars, and status badges.

Smart Schedule

View your week at a glance with a timetable that syncs your classes, events, and deadlines.

CWA & Analytics

Calculate your CWA, track grades per course, and visualise your academic performance over time.

Deadline Reminders

Get browser notifications at 48h, 24h, 12h, 3h, and 1h before every deadline so nothing slips.

Office Suite

Built-in Word, Excel, and PowerPoint editors. Import and export real .docx, .xlsx, and .pptx files.

PDF Tools

Merge, split, compress, watermark, rotate, and convert PDFs — all directly in your browser.
